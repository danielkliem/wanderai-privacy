Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

Open PathAIve, take a photo to trigger the paywall, and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom.

Why can't the AI identify my landmark?

Make sure GPS is enabled, hold your phone steady, and frame the landmark clearly. Move closer if needed.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > PathAIve > Cancel. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

What languages are supported?

PathAIve currently supports English, German, and Spanish for both the app interface and audio tours.

Does PathAIve store my photos?

No. Photos are processed in real time and never stored on our servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.
