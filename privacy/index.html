PathAIve - Immersive Audio Tour Guide

Last Updated: March 14, 2026 | Effective Date: March 14, 2026

1. Overview

PathAIve ("App", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Camera Data

  • What: When you use the camera feature, PathAIve accesses your device's camera to capture images of landmarks and locations.
  • How it's used: Images are sent to Firebase AI / Google AI models for landmark identification and tour-guide generation.
  • Storage: Images are NOT stored on PathAIve servers. They are processed only to fulfill your request and are handled under the applicable provider policies.
  • Your control: You control when the camera is used.

2.2 Photos in Your Gallery

  • What: When you save photos, the image is stored in your device's native photo gallery.
  • How it's stored: Photos are stored only on your device. Never uploaded to PathAIve servers.
  • Your control: Delete photos anytime from your gallery.

2.3 Location Data

  • What: The app may request your location for landmark context.
  • How it's used: To provide location-specific tour guide information.
  • Storage: Location data is processed locally. Not stored on our servers.

2.4 User Preferences

  • What: Your interests (history, architecture, art, nightlife, food).
  • Storage: Saved only on your device using local storage.
  • Your control: Modify or reset anytime in app settings.

2.5 Account & Authentication Data

  • What: If you sign in, Firebase Authentication may process your anonymous account ID, email address, display name, and authentication tokens.
  • How it's used: To support Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, and purchase/account continuity across sessions.
  • Storage: Authentication data is handled by Firebase Authentication rather than a separate PathAIve account backend.

2.6 Spoken Follow-Up Audio

  • What: If you use spoken follow-up questions, short audio clips, related transcripts, and prompts may be processed.
  • How it's used: To transcribe your question, generate an answer, and optionally synthesize a spoken reply.
  • Storage: PathAIve does not store these audio clips on its own servers. Temporary files may exist on your device during playback and processing.

2.7 Subscription Data

  • What: Payment and subscription information.
  • Handling: Managed by RevenueCat. PathAIve does not store payment data.
